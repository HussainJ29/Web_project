SKYNET FOOD FINDER

Food Finder is a technological solution that aims to enhance restaurant purchases and increase the profitability of suppliers. It markets near maturity products with promotional prices to combat waste and promote sustainability. It also connects food suppliers to food service and retail for the advancement of mutual gains, through advertising andnegotiation strategies that result in greater speed, transparency, and savings in commercial transactions and contributing to the prevention of food waste and preservation of the environment.
